RAISER BUILDERS CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

Raisers Construction Builder (RCB) provides comprehensive pre-construction and construction services and solutions to the clients. Our services are customized according to the complexity of the project, to satisfy the client’s needs.

Pre Construction services

Our approach to pre-construction is a disciplined and systematic method of optimizing every aspect of construction, from cost, schedule and quality, to safety and site logistics. We identify each project’s unique challenges and solve them in advance, using a combination of advanced computer modelling tools and old-fashioned construction know-how. Throughout the pre-construction service phase, Raisers Construction Builder staff provide guidance on selection and procurement of materials, building systems, equipment and methods of project monitoring. We evaluate and make recommendations on the availability of materials and labour, time requirements for procurement and factors related to cost. No matter the size or complexity of the job, Raisers Construction Builder thorough pre-construction services make every project the best it can be. Our pre-construction services are informed by deep in-house resources, both computers aided and manual.

Construction Services

Raisers Construction Builder is known for having ability to deliver projects with exceptional quality on time and budget. Through the use of our extensive tools and plants and with the efforts of subcontractors, suppliers and our professional teams, we provide the necessary labour, materials, tools, equipment, temporary utilities and facilities to complete construction of a project.
